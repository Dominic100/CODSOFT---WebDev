Projects

IoT-based Smart Pill Dispenser

The IoT-based Smart Medicine Pill Dispenser is like your personal medication buddy, especially designed to help out our elderly and those struggling with memory. It takes the hassle out of managing medications, ensuring they're taken on time, every time.

So, here's the deal: we brought together some cool IoT tech with a nifty dispenser device and a handy mobile app. This dispenser knows its stuff - it's programmed to pop out your meds exactly when you need them, all based on your schedule. And that app? It's like your medication planner on the go. You can set up your med schedule, get reminders, and even let your caregivers or docs peek in remotely to make sure you're staying on track.

We wanted to keep things simple and easy to use, so we mixed in both hardware and software magic. Our goal? To make life a bit easier for folks dealing with medication routines and ultimately, to improve their well-being. This project is all about using tech to tackle real-life health challenges and make a positive difference in people's lives.
